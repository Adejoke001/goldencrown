RCCG Mission
OFFICIAL RCCG MISSION STATEMENT

The Redeemed Christian Church of God Mission

To make heaven.

We are committed to teaching and living by the Word of God, ensuring that every member of our church understands the path to salvation and eternal life through Jesus Christ.

To take as many people with us.

We actively evangelize and disciple people from all walks of life, nations, and cultures, bringing them into the family of God and equipping them for service in His kingdom.

To have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations.

Through strategic church planting, missionary work, and discipleship programs, we aim to establish a vibrant Christian presence in every community worldwide.

"To accomplish this mission, we will plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries, and within five minutes driving distance in every city and town of developed countries."

RCCG Constitution - Article 4
OUR SPIRITUAL LEADER

Pastor E.A. Adeboye

General Overseer of The Redeemed Christian Church of God Worldwide

A Man of God Leading Global Revival

Pastor Enoch Adejare Adeboye, fondly called "Daddy G.O." by members, has been the General Overseer of RCCG since 1981. Under his leadership, RCCG has grown from about 200 parishes to over 40,000 parishes in 170 countries.

A former mathematics professor, Pastor Adeboye answered God's call to full-time ministry and has since dedicated his life to preaching the gospel, mentoring spiritual leaders, and expanding God's kingdom globally.

GOLDEN CROWN CHURCH SPECIFIC VISION

Our Local Mission & Vision

As an RCCG Zone, we have specific goals tailored to our community while aligning with the global RCCG vision

Community Transformation

To actively participate in community development, providing spiritual guidance, social support, and practical assistance to uplift our local community.

  • Monthly community outreach
  • Food and clothing drives
  • Counseling services

Discipleship & Growth

To raise committed disciples of Christ through systematic Bible teaching, prayer meetings, and fellowship that fosters spiritual growth.

  • Weekly Bible studies
  • Discipleship classes
  • Prayer and fasting programs

Church Planting

To plant new parishes within our zone, expanding our reach and making worship accessible to more people in our region.

  • Annual church planting goals
  • Leadership training
  • Resource mobilization

Join Us in Fulfilling Our Mission

Whether through prayer, volunteering, giving, or simply attending our services, you can be part of what God is doing through Golden Crown Church. Together, we can impact our community and the world for Christ.

OUR GUIDING PRINCIPLES

Core Values of Golden Crown Church

These values guide everything we do as a church community

Prayer & Worship

We prioritize prayer as our foundation and worship as our lifestyle, seeking God's presence in all we do.

Service & Compassion

Following Christ's example, we serve our community with compassion, meeting both spiritual and practical needs.

Biblical Integrity

We uphold the Bible as God's infallible Word and our ultimate authority in doctrine and practice.

Family & Unity

We foster a sense of family and unity, welcoming all people and building strong relationships grounded in love.
