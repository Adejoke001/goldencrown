As part of the Redeemed Christian Church of God Worldwide under the leadership of our Father in the Lord, Pastor E.A. Adeboye, we continue the mission to make heaven, take as many people with us, and have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations.

With over 40,000 parishes in 170+ nations, RCCG represents one of the fastest-growing Pentecostal denominations worldwide, committed to spreading the gospel and transforming communities.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Questions & Answers

Find answers to common questions about our church, faith, and services

Our Sunday services are held every Sunday from 8:30 AM to 11:30 AM. We also have an evening service at 5:00 PM on Tuesdays and Thursdays.

To become a member, we invite you to:

  1. Attend our Sunday service
  2. Complete a membership form
  3. Participate in water baptism if you haven't been baptized
  4. Join a house fellowship cell group

Visit our welcome desk on Sunday for more information.

We're excited to welcome you! Here's what to expect:

  • Friendly greeters at the entrance
  • Dynamic praise and worship (30 minutes)
  • Scripture reading and announcements
  • Powerful, Bible-based sermon (45-60 minutes)
  • Altar call and prayer ministry
  • Fellowship time after service

We conduct water baptism quarterly. Here's the process:

  1. Attend our baptismal class (2 sessions)
  2. Complete the baptism application form
  3. Have a brief interview with a pastor
  4. Participate in the baptism service

Baptism services are usually held on the last Sunday of the month. Contact the church office for the next baptism date.

Salvation is a simple but profound decision. Here's how:

Pray this prayer sincerely:

"Lord Jesus, I confess I am a sinner. I believe you died for my sins and rose again. Forgive me of my sins. Come into my heart and be my Lord and Savior. Thank you for saving me. In Jesus' name, Amen."

If you prayed this prayer, please let us know so we can support your spiritual journey! Visit the altar after any service or contact our prayer line.
