FOUNDED ON GOD'S PURPOSE

The RCCG Mission

Our mission as part of the Redeemed Christian Church of God Worldwide

To make heaven.

We are committed to teaching and living by the Word of God, ensuring that every member understands the path to salvation and eternal life through Jesus Christ.

To take as many people with us.

We actively evangelize and disciple people from all walks of life, nations, and cultures, bringing them into God's family and equipping them for kingdom service.

To have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations.

Through strategic church planting, missionary work, and discipleship programs, we aim to establish a vibrant Christian presence in every community worldwide.

"To accomplish this mission, we will plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries, and within five minutes driving distance in every city and town of developed countries."

RCCG Constitution - Article 4
PROPELLING FORWARD

Our Vision

Propelling forward with divine purpose and strategic growth

"To build a thriving, multi-generational church family that transforms communities through the power of the Gospel, raising kingdom leaders who impact every sphere of society."

Golden Crown Church Vision

RCCG Zone - 2025 and Beyond

Our Prophetic Mandate

God has called Golden Crown Church to be a beacon of hope and transformation in our generation. We are commissioned to raise a people of purpose, power, and praise who will establish God's kingdom in every territory we are planted.

Our vision extends beyond the four walls of our church building into homes, workplaces, schools, and government institutions - transforming society through kingdom principles.

Our Strategic Focus

We are strategically positioned to reach the unreached, disciple the believers, and deploy kingdom ambassadors into every sector of society. Our focus is on holistic transformation - spiritual, social, economic, and cultural.

By 2030, we envision Golden Crown Church as a model RCCG zone, impacting not just our immediate community but serving as a resource center for church planting and leadership development across our region.

Join Us in Fulfilling Our Mission

Whether through prayer, volunteering, giving, or simply attending our services, you can be part of what God is doing through Golden Crown Church. Together, we can impact our community and the world for Christ.

OUR SPIRITUAL LEADER

Pastor E.A. Adeboye

General Overseer of The Redeemed Christian Church of God Worldwide

A Man of God Leading Global Revival

Pastor Enoch Adejare Adeboye, fondly called "Daddy G.O." by members, has been the General Overseer of RCCG since 1981. Under his leadership, RCCG has grown from about 200 parishes to over 40,000 parishes in 170 countries.

A former mathematics professor, Pastor Adeboye answered God's call to full-time ministry and has since dedicated his life to preaching the gospel, mentoring spiritual leaders, and expanding God's kingdom globally.
